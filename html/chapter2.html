    • \(x^{\ast}A^{\ast} = \overline{x^{T}A^{T}} = \overline{(Ax)^{T}} = (Ax)^{\ast}\)

    • \(A^{\ast}Ax = 0\) implies \(x^{\ast}A^{\ast}Ax = (Ax)^{\ast}Ax = ||Ax||^2 = 0\) which implies \(Ax = 0\)
